About Us

The Redeemed Youth is an organization dedicated to reaching young people with the gospel, reviving their passion for God, and empowering them to live purposeful lives. Through discipleship, mentorship, conferences, and outreach programs, we equip the next generation to stand firm in faith and influence society positively.

Our Programs

Reach

Evangelism, school outreaches, and community engagements designed to bring young people into a saving relationship with Christ.

Revive

Prayer meetings, revival conferences, and teaching sessions that stir spiritual hunger and intimacy with God.

Empower

Leadership training, mentorship, and skill development to prepare young people for ministry, career, and life.

Media

Sermons, conference highlights, photos, and videos from our programs will be shared here to inspire and strengthen your faith.
